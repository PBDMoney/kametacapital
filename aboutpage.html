About

The first iteration of Kameta Capital was created in November 2017 focusing primarily on the cryptocurrency markets. In March 2020 seeing unprecedented opportunity due to a black swan event, we launched our equities and derivatives arm. At the time our original research and rigorous analysis allowed us to take advantage of a major equity market overreaction and subsequent gamma trap. Since then our AUM has grown substantially. Currently Kameta Capital focuses on public equity and derivative markets.
